# Sovereign.os Video Production Prompts

This file turns the short video campaign into reusable prompts for AI video, motion design, editing, and production teams.

## Universal visual direction

Use emotionally realistic scenes, not sci-fi AI visuals.

**Style:** quiet cinematic realism, natural light, muted interiors, close-up human moments, shallow depth of field, phone screens, pauses, silence, subtle body language.

**Avoid:** robots, glowing brains, holograms, cyberpunk graphics, therapy-office clichés, exaggerated crying, melodrama, medical imagery, or supernatural symbolism.

**Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical first. Create 1:1 and 16:9 crops after the vertical edit works.

**Color and texture:** warm neutral rooms, soft shadows, restrained contrast, realistic skin tones, minimal interface overlays.

**Pacing:** human pause first, brand line second, product name last.

## Prompt structure

Use this structure when creating generative video prompts:

```text
[Scene subject and setting]. [Emotional state shown through body language]. [Camera movement]. [Lighting]. [Texture and realism]. [No dialogue unless needed]. [No visible brand logos except end card]. [9:16 vertical].
```

## Voiceover direction

- Speak slowly.
- Leave air between lines.
- No influencer cadence.
- No clinical authority tone.
- The voice should sound like someone naming the thing the viewer already felt.

## Music direction

- Sparse ambient piano, soft analog pad, or near-silence.
- Avoid triumphant startup music.
- Avoid therapy commercial music.
- Avoid heavy cinematic trailer drums.

## Export checklist

For each clip, export:

- 9:16 with captions burned in.
- 9:16 clean version without captions.
- 1:1 crop for feed.
- 16:9 crop for landing page or YouTube.
- Thumbnail frame with headline text.
- Plain-text caption and CTA from `caption-pack.md`.

# Sovereign.os Launch Checklist

A lightweight operating plan for launching the Sovereign.os marketing campaign with grounded, non-clinical language.

## Pre-launch checklist

- [ ] Homepage copy uses the campaign center: "You are not broken. You are patterned."
- [ ] Campaign page is live and links to valid CTAs.
- [ ] Primary CTA is consistent: "Start with your baseline."
- [ ] Secondary CTA points to an existing route, such as "Open Defrag."
- [ ] Mobile QA covers hero, navigation, CTA stack, and campaign cards.
- [ ] Metadata, Open Graph, and Twitter card previews are verified.
- [ ] Accessibility check covers heading order, keyboard navigation, link labels, and color contrast.
- [ ] Deployment verification confirms the homepage and campaign page render after deploy.

## Video production checklist

- [ ] Scripts approved against the safe language standard.
- [ ] Voiceover recorded.
- [ ] B-roll generated or filmed.
- [ ] Captions burned in.
- [ ] Thumbnail exported.
- [ ] 9:16, 1:1, and 16:9 versions exported.

## Comment-response guidelines

- Mirror the person's observable language without labeling them.
- Use pattern, pressure, loop, repair, baseline, alignment, and relational intelligence.
- Keep replies short, calm, and non-clinical.
- If someone asks whether this is therapy, answer that Sovereign.os does not diagnose or treat; it helps people notice patterns and choose repair.
- If someone describes crisis or immediate danger, do not troubleshoot in comments; encourage qualified local support or emergency services.

## Metrics to track

- Hook retention
- Watch-through rate
- Profile clicks
- Waitlist or start CTA clicks
- Comments containing "this is me"

## Safety review

- [ ] No therapy, diagnosis, or treatment claims.
- [ ] No promises of healing.
- [ ] No claims about trauma cure.
- [ ] No destiny, divine-design, or spiritual-authority language.

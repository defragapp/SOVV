# Sovereign.os Short Video Campaign

## Campaign title

**You Are Not Broken. You Are Patterned.**

## Campaign purpose

Introduce Sovereign.os as a personal operating system for relational intelligence: a way to see, name, and repair the patterns behind reactions, pressure, conflict, avoidance, over-explaining, and disconnection.

The campaign should make people feel recognized before it explains the product.

## Core positioning

Sovereign.os is not another productivity assistant.

It is a relational intelligence platform that helps people understand the hidden operating system underneath how they protect, communicate, choose, withdraw, return, and repair.

## Core tagline options

- You are not broken. You are patterned.
- Defrag the pattern. Return to alignment.
- Understand what got touched before you react.
- The personal operating system for relational intelligence.
- See the loop. Name the pattern. Choose the repair.

## Tone

Quiet, precise, human, emotionally recognizable.

The product should feel like a mirror, not a chatbot.

Avoid futuristic AI tropes. Avoid robots, neon brains, holograms, and generic productivity dashboards.

Use real rooms, real phones, real silence, real pauses, and real emotional friction.

## Language guardrails

Use observable human language.

Do not diagnose. Do not imply treatment. Do not promise healing. Do not claim the product knows a person's soul, destiny, trauma, or medical state.

Good language:

- "The reaction starts before the reply."
- "The same loop is running again."
- "This moment touched pressure, protection, or old expectation."
- "Pause before the pattern speaks for you."

Avoid:

- "This is your trauma response."
- "Sovereign.os heals your childhood wounds."
- "Your soul is designed to..."
- "The system diagnoses your patterns."

## Editing notes

- Use 9:16 vertical first.
- Keep most cuts between 1.0 and 2.5 seconds.
- Keep end cards under 2 seconds.
- Let silence work; do not over-score the edits.
- Captions should be large, centered, and sparse.
- Faces and pauses matter more than UI mockups.

## Conversion path

Each clip should point to one of three actions:

- Join the waitlist.
- Try Defrag.
- Learn your Baseline.

For early launch, use one CTA consistently:

```text
Start with your baseline.
```

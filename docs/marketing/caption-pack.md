## Comment reply prompts

Use these to reply from the brand account when comments resonate.

### If someone says "this is me"

That moment of recognition is the starting point. The goal is not shame — it is seeing the loop clearly enough to choose differently.

### If someone asks "is this therapy?"

No. Sovereign.os is not therapy and does not diagnose or treat. It is a relational intelligence platform for noticing patterns, reflecting on pressure, and choosing repair.

### If someone asks "how does it work?"

Sovereign.os starts with your baseline, then helps translate pressure, conflict, and recurring loops into clear, usable language.

### If someone says "AI cannot understand humans"

That is the right concern. Sovereign.os is designed to avoid diagnosis, prediction, and identity labels. The goal is not to replace human wisdom — it is to help people see patterns more clearly.

# Sovereign.os Marketing

This directory contains campaign materials for Sovereign.os launch creative.

## Files

- `sovereign-os-short-video-campaign.md` — complete short-form video campaign brief with scripts, shot lists, voiceovers, on-screen text, end cards, launch order, and editing notes.
- `video-production-prompts.md` — reusable generative video prompts, b-roll prompts, motion text prompts, end-card direction, music direction, and export checklist.
- `caption-pack.md` — organic captions, paid ad hooks, CTAs, hashtags, thumbnail headlines, and comment reply prompts.
- `launch-checklist.md` — pre-launch, production, publishing, metrics, and safety review checklist.
- `video-asset-manifest.json` — structured manifest for the seven short-video concepts.
- `video-asset-manifest.md` — guidance for using the manifest in an editing or AI-video workflow.

## Product-space map

- **Defrag** — see the loop, name the pattern, choose the repair.
- **Alignment** — notice when you leave yourself and return to your true signal.
- **Covenant** — reveal what a relationship keeps repeating and where repair can begin.
- **Baseline** — understand how you move through pressure, choice, conflict, care, and repair.

## Safe language standard

Sovereign.os marketing should use observable, grounded language.

Do not frame the product as therapy, diagnosis, treatment, prediction, destiny, spiritual authority, or a replacement for human relationship.

The product promise is recognition and usable reflection: helping people see patterns clearly enough to choose repair.

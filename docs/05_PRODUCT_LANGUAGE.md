## Space Language

Defrag and Covenant are **spaces** inside Sovereign.os. They are not:
- Workbenches
- Workspaces (when referring to the space itself)
- Standalone products
- Separate platforms

**Correct:**
- "Enter the Defrag space."
- "Covenant is an optional reflection space."
- "Both spaces share your Baseline Design and Library."

**Incorrect:**
- "Enter the DEFRAG Workbench."
- "Open Workspace."
- "Covenant is a separate product."

```markdown
# Phase 17: Baseline Design (Premium Onboarding Flow)

## Core Objective
Build the one-time onboarding flow that captures a new user's "Baseline Design." This must feel like a high-end diagnostic tool, utilizing a full-screen takeover and introducing a subtle visual asset (an SVG topology or radar map) that reacts as they answer.

## Strict System Constraints
1. **Focus Mode:** The flow is a full-screen takeover (`fixed inset-0 z-50 bg-[#08070a]`).

- **Step 2:** `[02/03] CORE BOUNDARY` -> "What is the line that, when crossed, collapses your clarity?"

- **Routing:** If a user logs in and `has_baseline` is false, force-route them to `/setup`. 
- **Completion:** On Step 3 submission, POST the data to your backend, set the flag to true, and use the spatial Z-axis transition to push them into Defrag.

```
